# VeytCore

**VeytCore** is the unified, self-hosted operational backbone for the Veyt brand. Designed to support a distributed team across Denmark, Canada, China, and Morocco, this platform centralizes lead management, invoicing, and marketing automation into a high-performance, secure environment.

## 🎯 Purpose
VeytCore replaces fragmented processes with a cohesive, local-first CRM system. It allows our global team to maintain consistent workflows while ensuring compliance with European market standards and high-volume business growth.

## 🚀 Key Capabilities
* **Integrated CRM**: Centralized management for leads and customer relationships.
* **Automated Operations**: Built-in support for invoicing, tax compliance, and automated document generation via MinIO.
* **Event-Driven Workflows**: Real-time background job processing powered by Inngest.
* **Scalable Architecture**: Dockerized infrastructure designed for rapid deployment across global locations.

## 🏗️ Technical Stack
* **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
* **Database**: PostgreSQL (Prisma ORM)
* **Storage**: MinIO (S3-compatible persistent storage)
* **Orchestration**: Docker Compose
* **AI-Ready**: Designed to support agentic workflows and automated business intelligence.

## 🛡️ Operational Philosophy
Built for high-volume supplement brand management, VeytCore prioritizes data sovereignty, security, and streamlined developer experience. Every feature is audited to prevent security vulnerabilities, ensuring that our customer data remains robust and compliant.
